We Are the Best! (2014) Director: Lukas Moodysson

Story
Cast
Crew

Stockholm 1982. A film about Bobo, Klara and Hedvig. Three girls aged 12 to 13. Girls who hang around on the streets, chatting. Girls who are fashionable and tough and strong and weak and confused and silly. Girls who have to look after themselves far too young. Cooking fish fingers in the toaster while mum’s at the pub. Starting a punk band without any instruments to play, even though everyone says punk is dead.
